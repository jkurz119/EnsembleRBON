REQUIRED DATA FILES
===================

Please copy the following .mat files to the data/ directory:

data/EIT_4/
-----------
- EIT_FineSamples1.mat
- EIT_FineSamples2.mat
- EIT_FineSamples1or2.mat
- FineGridPoints.mat

data/Ensemble/
-------------
- EIT_2_CoarseSamples.mat
- CoarseGridPoints.mat

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Copy these files from your original locations to the corresponding directories in data/
2. The files should be placed exactly as shown above
3. Once all files are in place, you can run: julia --project=. -t 20 main.jl
